My Story

It's no fun being the old lady in the classroom... but you got to do what you go to do!

I've been whittling away at my Information Systems degree for many years. I finally took a leap of faith and left my full-time job to return to school full-time. Since I hold an Associate's degree in Information Systems, I've already been working in the field for 13 years. However, I was having trouble finding a job with a different company without my Bachelor's degree. I also had moved over to the business side and was on a management track. I really enjoy the technical side and wanted to move back in that direction. I took 2 classes over the summer, am enrolled in 6 class this fall, which will leave me with only the Capstone class to take next semester.

My husband David and I have three kids and two dogs: Addison - 16, Jack - 14, Tucker - 5, Mo - 8 year old Yorkichon and Maya - 18 month old Golden Retriever. The last member of our family is Cousin Eddy - a 1983 camper.
